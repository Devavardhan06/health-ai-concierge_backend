Clinic Name: Sunrise Multispeciality Clinic

=== GENERAL INFORMATION ===

Clinic Overview:
Sunrise Multispeciality Clinic is a trusted healthcare provider serving the community since 2015.
We are committed to delivering quality healthcare services with compassion and excellence.
Our clinic is equipped with modern medical facilities and staffed by experienced healthcare professionals.
We maintain strict hygiene standards and patient confidentiality protocols.

Clinic Timings:
The clinic operates from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
The clinic is closed on Sundays and public holidays.
Last token for same-day appointments is issued at 5:30 PM.
The reception desk closes at 6:00 PM sharp.

=== CONSULTATION SERVICES ===

General Physician Services:
We provide consultations for fever, cold, cough, and viral infections.
Treatment for digestive issues, headaches, and body aches is available.
Annual health checkups and preventive health screenings are offered.
Prescription renewals and follow-up consultations are provided.

Specialist Consultations:
Specialist consultations are available for diabetes, cardiology, and orthopedics.
Diabetes management includes blood sugar monitoring and lifestyle counseling.
Cardiology services cover blood pressure management and heart health assessments.
Orthopedic consultations address joint pain, fractures, and mobility issues.

Pediatric Services:
Pediatric consultations are available for children below 14 years.
Vaccination services for infants and children are provided as per national immunization schedule.
Growth and development monitoring is offered during pediatric visits.
Treatment for common childhood illnesses like fever, diarrhea, and skin rashes is available.

Women's Health Services:
Antenatal checkups and pregnancy monitoring are available.
Postnatal care and breastfeeding support are provided.
General gynecological consultations are offered.
Pap smear tests can be scheduled during gynecology consultations.

=== APPOINTMENT SYSTEM ===

Appointment Booking:
Appointments can be booked online through the clinic website at www.sunriseclinic.com.

=== DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES ===

Laboratory Services:
The clinic offers complete blood count (CBC) tests.
Blood sugar testing (fasting and post-prandial) is available.
Lipid profile tests for cholesterol screening are provided.
Liver function tests and kidney function tests are available.
Urine analysis and stool examination services are offered.
Thyroid function tests (T3, T4, TSH) can be conducted.
Sample collection is done from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM for lab tests.
Most lab reports are available within 24-48 hours.

Imaging Services:
ECG (Electrocardiogram) services are available during clinic hours without prior appointment.
X-ray services are available for bone fractures, chest X-rays, and joint assessments.
X-ray reports are provided on the same day.
Ultrasound services are not available at this clinic.
CT scan and MRI services are not available at this clinic.

=== PHARMACY SERVICES ===

Medicine Availability:
An in-house pharmacy stocks commonly prescribed medicines.
Generic and branded medications are available.
Prescription medicines are dispensed only with valid doctor prescriptions.
Over-the-counter medications for common ailments are available.
The pharmacy operates during clinic hours from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Medicine Home Delivery:
Home delivery of medicines is available within 5 km radius.
Delivery charges apply for orders below Rs. 500.
Free home delivery for medicine orders above Rs. 500.
Home delivery is available Monday to Saturday.

=== EMERGENCY SERVICES ===

Emergency Policy:
Emergency and trauma services are not available at this clinic.
Patients requiring emergency care should visit City General Hospital, located 2 km away.
For medical emergencies, patients should call the national emergency number 108.
First aid for minor injuries is available at the clinic.

=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: Do I need to book an appointment in advance?
A: Appointments are recommended but walk-ins are accepted based on availability.

Q: What documents should I bring for my first visit?
A: Please bring a valid ID proof and any previous medical records if available.

Q: Can family members accompany patients?
A: One attendant per patient is allowed inside the consultation room.

Q: Are video consultations available?
A: Video consultations are not currently available at our clinic.

Q: Do you provide medical certificates?
A: Yes, medical certificates are provided by doctors when medically necessary.

Q: Is ambulance service available?
A: Ambulance services are not provided by the clinic. Please call 108 for emergency ambulance.

Q: Can I get my reports via email?
A: Yes, lab reports can be emailed to registered email addresses upon request.

Q: Do you treat dental problems?
A: Dental services are not available at our clinic. We can refer you to a dental specialist.

Q: Is physiotherapy available?
A: Physiotherapy services are not available at this clinic.

Q: Can I transfer my medical records from another clinic?
A: Yes, you can bring your previous medical records and we will maintain them in our system.
